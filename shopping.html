    Shopping Guide

    • Orchard Road, Luxury Shopping Paradise!

      Orchard Road is Singapore's most famous shopping street, home to international luxury brands and high-end malls including ION Orchard and Ngee Ann City. From luxury goods to designer brands, it offers everything in a comfortable and elegant shopping environment. During the annual GSS Great Sale in June-July, major brands offer amazing discounts, making it the best time for shopping sprees!

    • Marina Bay Sands, Iconic Shopping Experience

      Marina Bay Sands Shopping Mall is not just a luxury paradise but also Singapore's iconic shopping experience! It features flagship stores of top brands like Louis Vuitton, Chanel, and Prada, along with canal shopping areas and numerous high-end restaurants. While shopping, you can also enjoy views of the infinity pool and cityscape from the Sands Hotel, for a complete luxury experience.

    • Chinatown, Unique Souvenirs

      Chinatown is the best place to buy unique Singaporean souvenirs! Here you'll find Merlion statues, Nyonya jewelry, traditional Chinese medicine and other special gifts. Prices are reasonable, and you can enjoy bargaining. Don't forget to try the street food for a perfect combination of shopping and dining!

    • Bugis & Little India, Affordable Shopping

      Bugis Street and Little India are great places to find affordable goods and unique items. Bugis is known for electronics and budget clothing, while Little India offers exotic Indian saris, spices and handicrafts. Both areas are vibrant and budget-friendly, perfect for cost-conscious shoppers.

    Singapore Shopping Highlights

    Singapore offers everything from luxury goods to affordable finds, with comfortable environments and abundant choices. Popular shopping areas include Orchard Road (international brands), Marina Bay Sands (luxury + landmark experience), Chinatown (unique souvenirs) and Bugis/Little India (affordable goods). The annual GSS Great Sale runs June-July, and you can get 5-8% tax refund on single purchases over S$100!
