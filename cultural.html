This is the cultural experiences page

This interactive project features a rule-based chatbot designed to guide users in making choices within travel and food-related scenarios. The bot offers a multi-path dialogue experience through predefined options (such as "visit attractions" or "try local food"), where clicking on different options leads users to corresponding video segments or content modules.

Closely aligned with the website's theme of "Singapore Travel and Food Exploration," it encourages active user participation, enhancing immersion and interactivity. Unlike AI-driven systems, this chatbot operates on a clear rule-based structure, where each branching option triggers a specific content path, deepening users' understanding and interest in the destination.
